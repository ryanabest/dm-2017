#17: Bible Verse


It’s hot!

1I can’t get cool,
I’ve drunk a
quart of lemonade.
I think I’ll
take my shoes off
And sit around
in the shade.

It’s hot!

10My back is sticky.
The sweat rolls
down my chin.
I think I’ll
take my clothes off
And sit around
in my skin.

It’s hot!

19I’ve tried with
’lectric fans,
And pools and
ice cream cones.
I think I’ll
take my skin off
And sit around
in my bones.

It’s still hot!


“It's Hot“ - Shel Silverstein
