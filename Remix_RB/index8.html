#8: Poem and Rhyme Scheme

It’s



hot!









I can’t get cool,
I’ve drunk a quart of
lemonade.
I think I’ll take my shoes off
And sit around in the
shade.








It’s



hot!









My back is sticky.
The sweat rolls down my
chin.
I think I’ll take my clothes off
And sit around in my
skin.








It’s



hot!









I’ve tried with ’lectric fans,
And pools and ice cream
cones.
I think I’ll take my skin off
And sit around in my
bones.








It’s still



hot!





“It's Hot“ - Shel Silverstein
